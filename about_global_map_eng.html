Titan TMG Machine Tool,
Inc. North America

is The Yared Group’s North American marketing arm for machine tools and contract manufacturing. Administration and field service are based in Akron, Ohio. The sales force is composed of experienced, factory sales personnel (Regional Sales Managers) located throughout the United States. Contract manufacturing services are sold on a direct basis. Machine tools are sold through exclusive distributors serving their local areas. Please contact Akron headquarters to determine your area distributor.

McNeil & NRM, Inc.-Akron,Ohio
McNeil & NRM-California, Inc.

The Yared Group includes two important manufacturers of tire industry equipment: McNeil & NRM, Inc.-Akron,Ohio and McNeil & NRM-California, Inc. McNeil & NRM, Inc. designs and markets tire curing presses and tire assembly machinery. The long and distinguished history of McNeil & NRM spans more than nine decades and scores of technological advancements. NRM and McNeil were both pioneers in tire production equipment; founded in 1907 and 1910 respectively. Throughout the ensuing decades, both companies became respected as innovators in the tire industry. The combined McNeil & NRM continues its commitment to research and development, dedication to product excellence, and its pursuit of total customer satisfaction. McNeil & NRM – California, Inc. designs and markets strip winding equipment for the tire, tire retread, and roll covering industries. Once a subsidiary of AMF Corporation and later The Goodyear Tire & Rubber Company, it is now part of The Yared Group.

TMG Machine Tools Factory, Bucharest Romania

For nearly 40 years, Titan Masini Grele (TMG) has been the technological centerpiece of the well known and respected Romanian machine tool industry. Experts from within the family of companies that make up The Yared Group have dedicated their skills and knowledge to the betterment of TMG. From 1997 to the present, they have integrated six separate Romanian companies into one company that offers true value for the customer. TMG’s nearly 600,000 square-foot campus (60,000 square meters) houses manufacturing space for fabrication and heat treatment, machining and assembly, office space for engineering, quality control and administration. It serves as the manufacturing facility for The Yared Group’s proprietary machinery products (Titan TMG machine tools and McNeil & NRM tire machinery) and provides valuable contract manufacturing services to customers worldwide.
