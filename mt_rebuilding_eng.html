Rebuilding, modification and CNC retrofitting of large machine tools, regardless of brand name, is commonly performed by TMG.
TMG provides high accuracy machining of critical parts such as guideways, gears, and reducer housings. Hydraulic work might range from a simple rebuild to a complete redesign of the entire system. TMG provides all electrical work from troubleshooting to full CNC conversion.
Retrofitting and modernization can result in CNC control of 3 to 5 axes, automatic tool changing, implementation of special milling heads, turning tables, CNC bi-rotary milling head, and part measuring probe. A complete retrofit by TMG provides a CNC machine tool with improved accuracy, reliability, and increased production efficiencies.
TMG also provides a variety of new, smaller machine tools, components and accessories for your shop.

TMG provides a full line of grinders including:
  • External universal grinding machines
  • Internal circular grinders
  • Crankshaft grinders
  • Camshaft grinders
  • External spline grinders
  • Internal spline grinders
  • Thread grinders
  • Jig grinding machines
  • Jig boring machines

TMG designs and manufactures displaceable rotary tables in sizes from 63"x71" to 138"x138" (1600x1800 mm to 3500x3500 mm) with load capacity to 100 tons.
